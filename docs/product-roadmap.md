# Book Quest Product Roadmap

## Vision

A lifelong self-learning platform where each learner has a persistent AI tutor that adapts to their goals, interests, and performance.

## MVP scope

- Onboarding interview with learner profile and preferred tutor character.
- Course creation from uploaded PDFs or AI-generated curriculum.
- Gated progression through units.
- Homework, quizzes, midterm, final.
- Correct/incorrect grading for MCQ + free-form responses.
- Concept mastery tracking and adaptive reinforcement.

## Post-MVP

- Rich tutor chat around incorrect answers.
- Better explainability and remediation plans.
- Community-contributed course packs.
